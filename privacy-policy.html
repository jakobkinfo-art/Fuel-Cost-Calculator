Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information the website may process, why it is used, how calculator settings are handled, and how to contact us.

Privacy summary

The calculators can be used without an account. Core calculations run in your browser. The website may still receive analytics, technical logs, locally stored preferences, URL parameters, and information you choose to send by email.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Fuel Cost Calculator (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you visit fuel-cost-calculator.com, use our calculators, follow links, or contact us. It does not govern third-party websites or services that have their own privacy policies.

2. Information we may collect

Calculator inputs

Core calculator inputs are generally processed in your browser to produce a result. Some calculator settings may be added to the page URL to support sharing or reopening a calculation. URL parameters can appear in browser history, server logs, analytics systems, referrer information, screenshots, or any message in which you share the URL.

Analytics and device information

We may collect or receive information such as page views, approximate location derived from an IP address, browser and device type, operating system, referral page, interaction events, error information, and timestamps. We use this information to understand usage, maintain security, diagnose problems, and improve the website.

Technical logs

Our hosting, security, content-delivery, and domain providers may process IP addresses, request details, timestamps, user-agent information, and security signals in server or network logs.

Information you provide

If you email us, we receive your email address and any name, page URL, calculator values, message content, attachments, or other information you choose to include.

3. How we use information

  • Provide and operate calculators, guides, and website features.
  • Measure usage and improve design, content, accessibility, and performance.
  • Detect abuse, prevent fraud, protect the website, and diagnose errors.
  • Respond to questions, privacy requests, suggestions, and error reports.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Google Analytics

The website may use Google Analytics or related Google measurement services. These services can use cookies or similar technologies and receive information about your device and interactions. Google's use of information is governed by its own terms and privacy documentation. Where required, analytics may be limited until you make a consent choice.

5. Hosting, security, and Cloudflare

The website may use Cloudflare for hosting, content delivery, DNS, performance, and security. Cloudflare may process technical request data to deliver pages, mitigate attacks, and maintain service reliability under its own privacy terms.

6. Cookies and local storage

We may use cookies, browser local storage, or similar technologies to remember consent choices, unit preferences, interface settings, or other site functionality. You can clear or block these technologies in your browser, although some preferences or features may stop working as expected.

7. How information may be shared

We may share information with vendors that provide hosting, analytics, email, security, or technical services; when required by law or valid legal process; to protect rights, safety, and service integrity; or as part of a business transfer. We do not sell personal information for money. We do not knowingly use calculator inputs to make decisions about credit, insurance, employment, housing, or eligibility.

8. Retention

Retention depends on the type and purpose of the information. Technical and analytics data may be retained according to provider settings and operational needs. Email correspondence may be retained while needed to respond, maintain records, resolve disputes, or meet legal obligations. Locally stored preferences remain on your device until they expire or you remove them.

9. International processing

The website is available internationally, and service providers may process information in countries other than your own. Those countries may have different privacy laws. Where applicable, providers may rely on recognized safeguards for international transfers.

10. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location and whether a particular law applies, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, or information about the handling of personal data. Residents of certain US states may also have rights to know, correct, delete, or opt out of certain sales, sharing, targeted advertising, or profiling.

We do not currently offer user accounts or a database of saved calculator profiles. To submit a privacy request, email info@fuel-cost-calculator.com. We may need enough information to understand and verify the request. You may also have the right to complain to a privacy regulator in your jurisdiction.

11. Children's privacy

The website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review the situation.

12. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect the website and information we handle. No internet transmission, browser storage, email system, or service provider can be guaranteed completely secure.

13. Third-party links and embeds

Pages may link to maps, government sources, fuel-economy resources, charging services, advertisers, affiliates, or other third parties. Their websites may collect information independently. Review their privacy policies before providing information.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when features, providers, legal requirements, or data practices change. The revised date will appear on this page. Material changes may also be highlighted on the website.

15. Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to info@fuel-cost-calculator.com.

Last updated July 21, 2026. This policy is a general website privacy notice and is not a substitute for advice from a qualified privacy professional about your specific legal obligations.
